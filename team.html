Chris Caputo

Co-founder & Managing Director

As a Co-founder and Managing Director of Fortress Group, Chris manages key investor relationships, originates new mandates, and plays an active role in project management. Chris co-founded Fortress Group as successor to the business he established in 1998, Fortress Capital Corp., a merchant banking firm specializing in private equity investments and capital raising for private equity funds. Prior to 1998, Chris was Managing Director and Co-Head of the Private Equity Group at Bank Austria Group. During his seven years at Bank Austria and its predecessor, Creditanstalt, Chris was instrumental in developing a private equity placement and merchant banking business. Prior to Bank Austria, Chris worked at Goldman Sachs & Co. and began his career at Bear, Stearns & Co. in the Corporate Finance Group. Chris received his B.A. in Economics from Colgate University and his MBA from the Tuck School of Business at Dartmouth. (Series 7, 63, 24, 79)

Bruce Williamson, CFA

Co-founder & Managing Director

As a Co-founder and Managing Director of Fortress Group, Bruce manages key investor relationships, originates new mandates, and plays an active role in project management. Prior to co-founding Fortress Group, Bruce was Managing Director of Williamson Advisors, an investment banking firm he founded in 1994 to offer M&A and capital advisory services to U.S. middle-market companies in an array of industries. Prior to Williamson Advisors, Bruce was Vice President at KD Equities, a New York-based buyout fund focused on middle-market companies. Previously, he was with L.F. Rothschild & Co. in Corporate Finance / M&A. He began his career at Bear, Stearns & Co. in the Financial Restructuring Group. Bruce received his A.B. degree from Harvard College and is a CFA Charterholder. (Series 7, 63, 24, 28, 79, 99, 50)
